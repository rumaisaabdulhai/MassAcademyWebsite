Drawing

Drawing has always been near and dear to my heart. It has become a way for me to de-stress when I have a lot of work. I like to draw people, animals, and flowers. Most of the time, I stick to an animated style of drawing, but I do enjoy a bit of realism. Someday, I hope to create a manga of my own. To the left is a drawing of me. Lovebirds

With my mom, I take care of twelve lovebirds, making them special food and playing with them. I raised six of them since they hatched out of their eggs, watching them grow from feeble, red-skinned babies to fluffy, noisy birds. Simply holding them on my finger and letting them fly in my house relieves me of all the accumulated stress from school.

Baking

I love making food for my friends and family. Usually, I bake cheesecakes and cupcakes for my sisters' and parents' birthdays. I also make lobster bisque with my dad. I've recently been making passion fruit boba and Indian chaats with my sister as a late night snack.

Robotics

My interest in robotics started in 6th grade when I visited the Liberty Science Center in New Jersey. After building a rubiks cube solver using Lego Mindstorms, I wanted to see what else I could do with existing parts I had at home and a 3D pen. In 8th grade, I built a 3D printer using Lego parts and a microcontroller. I learned a lot about how a 3D printer prints layer by layer.
