Advanced Immersion Spanish

Language is taught by Sra. Wildfong. At Mass Academy, students have the choice of taking either French or Spanish. For each language, there are advanced and intermediate counterparts. I am taking advanced Immersion Spanish as I have taken Spanish at my sending school. In class, we read interesting stories, learn about Spanish culture by listening to music or watching movies, and also learn a bit of grammar.





Laberinto del Fauno

During C Term, we watched Laberinto del Fauno by Guillermo del Toro in class. The story takes place after the Spanish Civil War, and it is about a young girl named Ofelia and her pregnant mother who stay with Ofelia's stepfather, Captain Vidal. Ofelia follows a fairy into a secret Labrynth and meets a faun who believes she is the lost Princess Moanna from the Underworld. The faun promises to return Ofelia to the Underworld only if she successfully completes 3 tasks. We had to write from the perspective of one of the characters in the story. I wrote from the perspective of the Doctor Ferreiro who works for the Captain but is secretly a rebel. You can also view the reflection here.
