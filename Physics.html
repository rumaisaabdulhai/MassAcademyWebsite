Advanced Physics with Calculus

Physics is taught by Mr. Ellis. It is one of my favorite classes as we get to work on challenging problems everyday. So far, we have learned principles in kinematics, dynamics, energy, and some concepts in rotational motion. For every unit, we usually do an Uber Problem, which involves many careful steps. Students have a choice of doing the calculus or algebra problem.
