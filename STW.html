Literature Review

Before working on any science or engineering project, it is essential to conduct research about the work being done in the field to understand what is already implemented and the current challenges researchers are facing. All of the preliminary research is captured in a Literature Review. Conducting a Literature Review gives a strong purpose to our STEM projects at Mass Academy.
