Hello!

I'm Rumaisa Abdulhai (left), a senior at the Massachusetts Academy of Math & Science (MAMS) in Worcester, Massachusetts. The picture on the left is me with my two sisters during my sister's wedding this past summer. I am passionate about physics, computer science, and robotics. In my free time, I love to draw and spend time with my family. I am grateful to have lived in 4 different countries- Qatar, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and the US, and attended 6 different schools before MAMS.



Beaver Works Summer Program

In the summer of 2019, I attended a 4-week intensive program called Beaver Works at MIT, where I learned how to program a research drone using Python and the Robot Operating System (ROS) to autonomously navigate an illuminated trajectory while avoiding AR tagged obstacles. The picture to the right is my team, and I am holding the drone we competed with. We were excited to win 1st place at the end of our program. You can read more about my experience here.



International Math Modeling

In April of 2020, I competed in the 5-day International Mathematical Contest in Modeling (IMMC) with 3 of my classmates. We developed simulations in Python and Java to maximize revenue in flash sales (like Black Friday) while minimizing damage to products. We were pleasantly surprised that we won the Finalist Prise for IMMC in the US! You can read more about my experience here.



My Birds

I take care of twelve beautiful lovebirds. We started with only two from a nice bird owner, and since then they've grown to a little flock! My birds love to eat apple and millet. They also love to chirp all day long, especially when they're sleepy. The picture on the right is my sister holding one of the birds when he was a baby.



Umrah Trip

In 2018, I went for Umrah (a pilgrimage) with my family to the cities of Makkah and Medinah in Saudi Arabia. It was my first time visiting after 10 years. I really enjoyed the experience, and met so many people of different cultures from around the world. The mosques were beautiful and I miss it so much. To the left is me with my sisters in front of the Kaaba, one of the holiest sites in Islam.
